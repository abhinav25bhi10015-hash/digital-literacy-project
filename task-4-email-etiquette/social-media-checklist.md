The 5-and-5 Rule for Students

Do: Use a professional profile picture on networking sites.

Do: Double-check your privacy settings regularly.

Do: Share projects or certifications that showcase your skills.

Do: Fact-check information before sharing it with others.

Do: Respect others' opinions even during online debates.

Don't: Post confidential information about yourself or your university.

Don't: Use offensive or unprofessional language in public comments.

Don't: Tag others in photos or posts without their permission.

Don't: Engage with "trolls" or participate in cyberbullying.

Don't: Click on suspicious links sent via direct messages.
