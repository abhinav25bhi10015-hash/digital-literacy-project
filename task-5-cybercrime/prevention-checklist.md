### Stay Safe Online: Student Prevention Guide

* **Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money:** A PIN is only required for sending or authorizing a payment, never for receiving.
* **Verify the 'Verified' badge:** Only interact with official business accounts on payment apps and double-check the recipient's name before hitting 'Send'.
* **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Activate 2FA on your email, LinkedIn, and social media accounts to add an extra layer of security.
* **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Create complex passwords for different platforms; avoid using easily guessable info like your birthdate, name, or phone number.
* **Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Sensitive Tasks:** Never log into bank accounts or enter credit card details while connected to free public Wi-Fi networks.
* **Check for HTTPS and the Padlock:** Ensure a website's URL starts with `https://` and shows a locked padlock icon before entering any personal information.
* **Beware of Phishing Links:** Do not click on links in SMS or emails that claim your account is "blocked" or "suspended" and ask for immediate action.
* **Know the Reporting Channel:** If you are a victim of a cybercrime, immediately call the **National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930** or report it at **cybercrime.gov.in**.
